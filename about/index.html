About — An independent practice

I design quiet, deliberate things — identities, books, posters, and the occasional website — for clients who value care over volume.

Biography

Jean Theilborie is a designer and art director based between Lisbon and Paris. The studio was founded in 2018 after five years at a larger agency, and has since worked with publishers, museums, restaurants, and small independent brands.

Trained as an illustrator before turning fully to design, Jean treats every project as a problem of language first — what is this thing, how does it speak, and who is it speaking to. Typography and restraint follow from there.

Available for a small number of projects each year. Usually identity, editorial, or print. Occasionally something that does not fit a category at all.
